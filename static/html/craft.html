CRAFT by World Market is a new program helping artisans around the world get their products to retail. World Market's buyers scour the far reaches of the globe to find these products, and if enough people pledge to purchase them, then they're produced.

I developed the front end for CRAFT, heavily utilizing the Handlebars templating engine, and data filtering using Underscore. I worked closely with the client's IT team and their e-commerce provider to develop the product data feeds that power the site. The result is a heavily dynamic application, driven entirely through external JSON.

Handlebars.js, Underscore, CSS3 animation, JSON services
