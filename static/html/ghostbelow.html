The Ghost Below website is the interactive arm of a larger art installation featured at the Marine Mammal Center in Sausalito, CA.

Through vertical scrolling and parallax effects, the site tells the story of ocean trash as it falls to the bottom of the ocean, and its impact on sealife along the way.

Additionally, site visitors can make a virtual "promise" to reduce their impact on the ocean, by recycling or reducing plastic water bottle usage. These promises are geolocated by IP address, and displayed on a map.

Parallax motion, PHP, MySQL, Google Maps API, IP geolocation
