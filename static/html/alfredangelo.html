Alfred Angelo is a nationally known manufacturer and retailer of bridal gowns and accessories. Their site, alfredangelo.com receives several hundred thousand unique visitors per month.

The site runs on a fully customized CMS, based on C#. Over the course of 5 years I built and the system from the ground up, from database design to middle-tier business logic to all front-end presentation.

The backend system allows admin users to manage just about every aspect of the site - including home page layouts, product uploads, product images, store data, events, . It also has with full roles and permissions and automated sales lead capture reports.

User accounts created on the website are also seamlessly integrated with the retail locations' POS systems, allowing store associates to retrieve a customer's account information in-store.

The site is also localized for 5 different regions — USA, Canada, UK, Europe, Australia — and supports entirely unique content in each.

C#, SQL Server, stored procedures, IIS URL Rewriting Engine, XML, XPath, Google Maps API, ExactTarget integration, Scene7, AS/400 integration
