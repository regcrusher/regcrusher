In 2013, Soy Vay wanted to do something big, memorable, and out-of-this-world to launch their brand of marinades in a new market. So, we shot a piece of marinated chicken into space—and cooked it. And we called it A Pollo '13.

The website puts users on the edge of space and serves as the informational hub for the launch as well as the party and concert that were part of the whole event. The experience begins in the far reaches of space, and uses parallax motion to descend back to Earth.
