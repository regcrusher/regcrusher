For the 2013 holiday season, we wanted to do things a little differently. We introduced a giving component to our agency's holiday card — in which the company made holiday donations to organizations that are close to our hearts.

We sent holiday cards to hundreds of our clients and friends. Each card had a unique code printed on it, instructing them to visit our holiday giving site and enter using their unique code. Once entered onto the site, a gorgeous snow-covered scene of our building is displayed — an impossibility in San Francisco. Users can click on the bows that show up in the office windows, with each bow representing a charitable cause. They can then pledge to support that cause, and then we made a donation in their name.

PHP, MySQL, CSS3 animations and transitions
